# Tech Stack & Strategy Brainstorm: Setu

Building a digital presence for a mental wellness practice requires a balance of **professionalism**, **trust**, **security**, and **cultural resonance**. Below are the recommended paths forward for "Setu".

## 🎯 Strategic Considerations

### 1. Privacy & Compliance (Crucial)
Since this is a health-adjacent service (Mental Wellness), data privacy is paramount.
*   **HIPAA/GDPR Compliance:** Even if not strictly required in all regions, following these standards builds immense trust.
*   **Secure Contact Forms:** Avoiding standard email for sensitive health info; using encrypted forms or secure portals.

### 2. Branding & Cultural Resonance ("Setu" - The Bridge)
The design should reflect the "Bridge" concept—connecting Indian heritage with modern clinical excellence. 
*   **Visuals:** Warm, earthy tones mixed with modern professional accents. Avoid "stock" western clinic photos; use culturally appropriate imagery or custom generated assets.
*   **Terminology:** As requested, focusing on "Mental Wellness" and "CBT/Person-Centered" clinical framework.

---

## 🛠️ Option 1: The "Premium Custom" Build (Selected)
This approach allows us to create a high-performance, unique website that stands out.

*   **Frontend:** [Next.js](https://nextjs.org/) (React) + [Vanilla CSS](https://developer.mozilla.org/en-US/docs/Web/CSS) or [Tailwind](https://tailwindcss.com/).
*   **CMS (Content Management):** [Sanity.io](https://www.sanity.io/) or [Contentful](https://www.contentful.com/). This allows the therapist to update services and posts without touching code.
*   **Hosting:** [Vercel](https://vercel.com/) (Fast, global delivery).
*   **Pros:** Extreme speed, SEO-friendly, completely unique design, no "templated" look.
*   **Cons:** Higher initial setup time compared to no-code.

---

## 🚀 Recommendation for "Setu" (Finalized Decision)
We are proceeding with a **Hybrid Next.js Strategy**:
1.  **Mantine + Tailwind CSS:** For a robust, premium, and manageable UI.
2.  **Shadcn/UI:** For custom, high-impact design elements.
3.  **Design Inspiration:** Based on warm, earthy WordPress mockups (Peach/Beige, Bold Orange).
